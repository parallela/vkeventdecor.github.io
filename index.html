Our Story

Welcome to VK Event Decor, where creativity meets elegance! Founded by the dynamic duo Vanessa and Kamelia, we specialize in transforming ordinary spaces into extraordinary experiences. With years of combined experience in event planning and decoration, we understand that every celebration is unique and deserves a personal touch.

Our passion lies in creating magical atmospheres that reflect your vision and exceed your expectations. From intimate gatherings to grand celebrations, we bring artistry, attention to detail, and a touch of luxury to every event we design.

Let us turn your special moments into unforgettable memories with our signature style of elegance, creativity, and personalized service.
